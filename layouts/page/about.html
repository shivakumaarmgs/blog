Background

How I got here

I started out building Rails apps for clients at UDProducts — foundational years where I learned to ship, communicate, and own things end to end.

Then at PromptCloud, I got to build products from zero: took JobsPikr from an idea to a distributed ElasticSearch pipeline handling 20M daily ingestions and nearly a million real-time queries. Owning a product — not just a feature — shaped how I think about both engineering and leadership.

Fintech found me through Solaris, and I haven't looked back. There's something genuinely interesting about building in a space where correctness isn't optional.
